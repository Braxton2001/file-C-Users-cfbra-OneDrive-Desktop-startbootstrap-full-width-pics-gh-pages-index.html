Doing Good, Together

People management and development

I believe in doing good, together. People have the capability of great innovation and creation, so why not create and innovative to make the world a better place and why not do it together? I believe there is good in everyone, and when we nurture that goodness, we as a collective planet win, together. I am a Human Resource management student at Brigham Young University, and I work as an HR Student Assistant at BYU Broadcasting. I am from Florida, and I love the beach. I am fluent in both English and Spanish. I am a Crossfit athlete and have competed in local Crossfit competitions. I love hiking and being outdoors. I am always excited to meet new people! Volunteer Work

Full-Time Missionary 2020-2022 | Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints

I served as a Full-time mission in the Utah Provo mission from 2020-2022. I served as a representative of the Church, actively engaging with individuals and communities to share the teachings and principles of the gospel of Jesus Christ. Learned Spanish and connected with local Latino community. Organized and participated in community service projects, addressing local needs and building connections within the community. I managed a demanding schedule, balancing teaching appointments, community outreach, and personal development activities. Regularly taught English classes to Spanish speakers. I served as the Social Media Engagement Manager for the Mission Facebook Page, Clases de Ingles Gratis en Utah, with over 100 English class referrals sent to around the world. -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Second Councilor of the Bishopric of the Provo YSA 94th ward | Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints

I currently serve as the Second Councilor of the Bishopric of the Provo YSA 94th ward.
